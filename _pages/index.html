There’s a reason why Thailand is such a well-loved travel destination, but don’t just take our word for it. On this trip, you’ll get up close to the floating food markets, explore ornate temples, try your hand at a cooking class and travel by tuk tuk along the bustling streets.

Then we'll depart the bustling cities and discover the natural wonders of Thailand. Swim through sea caves, kayak in blue lagoons and marvel at the limestone cliffs. After all the activity you might be in need of some rest and relaxation. Lucky for you Thailand is the perfect place to recline on a sun-soaked beach and let the worries of the world float away.
