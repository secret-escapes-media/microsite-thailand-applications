journey: - title: Your adventure begins in Bangkok content: >

There’s a reason why Thailand is such a well-loved travel destination, but don’t just take our word for it. On this trip, you’ll get up close to the floating food markets in Bangkok, explore ornate temples, try your hand at a cooking class and travel by tuk tuk along the bustling streets before you immerse yourself in Bangkok’s legendary nightlife.

- title: Relax in tropical Thailand content: >

Then we’ll send you south to the islands. First stop, Phuket. Adventure through sea caves, kayak in blue lagoons and marvel at the limestone cliffs that surround you. After all that activity, you might be in need of some rest and relaxation, and lucky for you, your last stop is dreamy Khao Lak where pristine beaches and crystal clear waters await. Take a boat trip out to sea and snorkel among the tropical fish. Lush nature is also on the cards with a bamboo rafting trip through the jungle where you’ll feel the spray of the majestic waterfalls.

Maybe it’s someone who lives halfway around the world who you haven’t seen for years and you want to reunite in one of the greatest places on earth?
